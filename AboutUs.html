Our Story

AMUSE Digital Studio was founded by three college students in Canada. While studying abroad in a country with different culture, they all felt lonely during those traditional holiday season and meaningful days taking place throughout the year.

Wanting to bring joy and happiness to others, so they created AMUSE, a passionate team that provides original, creative event management for individuals and groups. Their mission is to create refreshing, entertaining events for clients that can actually amuse people, not just acquire it.
